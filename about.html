About Me

I’m a Data Analyst & Business Intelligence Specialist who bridges the gap between raw data and strategic decisions. Over the past 3+ years, I’ve helped organizations across e-commerce, SaaS, and nonprofit sectors unlock growth, streamline operations, and gain data-driven clarity.

I’m passionate about turning complexity into simplicity, whether that’s building dashboards, automating workflows with Python and Power Query, or designing cloud-based analytics pipelines that scale. My work has boosted sales, uncovered millions in revenue, and saved countless hours of reporting time for global teams.

Beyond the numbers, I believe data should tell a story that inspires action and that we can manipulate data to give us all the secrets. That’s why I combine technical expertise with business insight, ensuring every dashboard, model, or report is not just accurate but impactful.

When I’m not deep in SQL queries or fine-tuning KPIs, you’ll find me exploring emerging tech, mentoring teams on data literacy, or finding ways analytics can drive positive change in underserved communities.
