Bus Transportation Analysis Interactive Excel Dashboard

Designed and developed an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze bus transportation trends, usage, and performance for operational decision-making. The dashboard transforms raw transportation data into actionable insights for better planning and resource allocation.

Key Insights:

  • Identified busiest and least busy routes (East-West Express most congested, South Line least congested).
  • Determined peak (8:57 PM) and off-peak (7:50 PM) operating hours.
  • Classified bus utilization: 24% over-utilized, 22% under-utilized, 45% moderately utilized, 9% well-utilized.
  • Revealed a year-over-year ridership decline of -83.5%.

Why This Matters: Supports route optimization, improves service efficiency, enhances customer satisfaction, and identifies opportunities for cost reduction and revenue growth.

Data Model: Built on a structured star schema in Excel Power Pivot with fact and dimension tables for efficient reporting and analysis.

Technologies: Microsoft Excel, Power Query, PivotTables, Data Visualization & Dashboard Design.

SQL Exploratory Data Analysis - Optimizing Restaurant Performance

Conducted an in‑depth SQL analysis to evaluate restaurant sales performance, customer preferences, and pricing strategies using data from menu_items and order_details tables. The analysis generated actionable insights to optimize menu offerings, refine pricing, and improve overall profitability.

Key Insights:

  • Italian dishes are the most expensive (avg. $16.75) while American dishes are the cheapest (avg. $10.07).
  • The Hamburger is the most ordered item (622 orders), while Chicken Tacos is the least ordered (123 orders).
  • High‑spending orders favor Italian cuisine, with the top order totaling $192.15 for 14 items.
  • 20 orders had more than 12 items, highlighting group or family purchasing patterns.

Recommendations: Introduce premium American menu options, optimize low‑performing items (e.g., Chicken Tacos), and create targeted loyalty programs for high‑spending customers.

Technologies: SQL (PostgreSQL/MySQL), Data Cleaning, Data Analysis, Joins, Aggregations.

Taxi Organization Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing taxi service operations over three months (May–July 2024). The project identifies key revenue patterns, trip behavior, driver efficiency, and external impacts such as weather and surge pricing. This interactive dashboard enables data-driven decisions to improve operations and enhance customer experience.

Key Insights:

  • Revenue grew from $6.3k in May to $7.1k in July, showing seasonal trends or operational improvements.
  • Peak revenue hours: 6 PM ($1,014), 10 AM ($945), and 2 PM ($941).
  • Uptown generated the highest revenue ($4.30k), while Suburbs contributed the least ($3.66k).
  • Snowy conditions led to the highest demand, generating $4.2k in revenue.
  • Long-distance trips accounted for 75% of revenue; short trips only 3%.

Recommendations: Optimize fleet allocation in Uptown during peak hours, promote long-distance trips, adjust surge pricing for key hours, and enhance short-trip profitability.

Technologies: Microsoft Power BI, DAX, CSV Data Processing, Data Visualization.

Covid‑19 Visualization – Tableau Dashboard

This interactive Tableau dashboard visualizes the global impact of the Covid‑19 pandemic, enabling users to explore trends in confirmed cases, recoveries, and fatalities over time. The dashboard provides both global and country‑specific insights, allowing for comparison across regions and tracking changes over key phases of the outbreak. Designed for decision‑makers and the public, it combines visual storytelling with data interactivity to reveal patterns, hotspots, and progress in pandemic response.

Key Insights:

  • Covid‑19 cases show distinct waves, with major surges concentrated in specific months.
  • Certain countries and regions consistently reported higher case counts, reflecting varying outbreak severity and public health responses.
  • Death rates varied significantly across regions, influenced by healthcare capacity, demographics, and policy measures.
  • Recovery rates improved over time as vaccination coverage expanded and treatment protocols evolved.
  • Some regions reported lower case counts likely due to limited testing and under‑reporting.

Technologies: Tableau, CSV Data Processing, Data Visualization, Interactive Dashboard Design.
